Privacy Policy

We believe in transparency. Here is exactly what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

🔐 What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to deliver the app experience:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Learning progress — XP earned, lessons completed, CEFR level, streak data, and quiz scores.
  • Favourite words and SRS data — words you save and their spaced-repetition scheduling metadata.
  • Journal entries — text you write in the in-app Croatian journal feature.
  • Voice recordings — short microphone clips you record in the speaking, pronunciation, and checkpoint features. These are sent off your device to be transcribed and scored (see “AI & Voice Processing” below) and are not retained after processing.
  • Photos you submit — if you use the photo-vocabulary feature, the photo you choose is sent to our AI provider to generate vocabulary; it is not retained afterwards.
  • Device/browser type — collected automatically by Firebase for crash reporting and performance monitoring.
We do not collect your full name, phone number, postal address, or payment details. Beyond your email address, the personal data we process is limited to the learning, journal, voice, and photo data described above — all used solely to operate the app.

🔁 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the app:

  • Cross-device sync — progress is saved to Firebase Firestore so it appears on any device you sign in to.
  • Personalisation — your CEFR level and completed lessons determine which content is shown.
  • Streak and XP tracking — powers gamification features that help you stay motivated.
  • App improvement — anonymised aggregate usage data (e.g. which lessons are most used) helps us improve content and fix bugs.

☁ Firebase & Data Storage

Naša Hrvatska is built on Google Firebase (Google LLC). Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Authentication, hosted on Google’s infrastructure.

Google Firebase complies with GDPR and participates in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. For details, see firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Firebase may store data on servers outside the European Economic Area. By using the app you acknowledge this transfer. Standard Contractual Clauses apply as required by GDPR Art. 46.

🤖 AI & Voice Processing

To power speaking practice, pronunciation scoring, AI feedback, and audio, we send certain data to third-party processors. They act only on our instructions, under data-processing agreements, and we do not retain your voice recordings or submitted photos after processing.

  • Voice / speech-to-text — microphone recordings are transcribed by OpenAI (Whisper), Deepgram, and/or Cloudflare Workers AI (Whisper); pronunciation is assessed by Microsoft Azure Speech Services.
  • AI feedback & scoring — text (and, for the photo-vocabulary feature, a photo you submit) is sent to Anthropic (Claude) to generate corrections, tutoring, and scores.
  • Text-to-speech — text you choose to hear is sent to ElevenLabs, Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud Text-to-Speech to generate audio.
  • Talking-avatar video — the D-ID service generates avatar video from text.
  • Support email — Resend delivers your support messages to us.
  • Analytics & error monitoring — Plausible, PostHog, and Sentry, configured to minimize personal data.
Your voice recordings and submitted photos are used solely to give you transcripts, feedback, and scores. They are not used to identify you, are not used to train AI models, and are not sold or shared for any commercial purpose.

🚫 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes — ever.

We do not run advertising. We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

👤 Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or EEA you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (“right to be forgotten”) — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction / Objection — ask us to limit or stop processing in certain circumstances.
  • Lodge a complaint — with your national data protection authority (e.g. AZOP in Croatia).
To delete your account and all data, go to Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email support@nasahrvatska.com. We will process requests within 30 days.

🔒 Security

Your data is protected by Firebase security rules, which ensure users can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Auth.
